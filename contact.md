<section class="join-projects">
  <p class="join-section-eyebrow">// Active research thrusts</p>
  <p>
    We have open positions across three areas: real-time user authentication
    and information verification for telepresence (sensor fusion, semantic
    analysis, homomorphic encryption, zero-knowledge proofs), light-weight
    sensor-based authentication for vehicular networks (V2X, ambient sensor
    signals, attack-resistant evaluation), and zero-interaction authentication
    for heterogeneous mobile and IoT devices (cross-hardware invariants and
    real-world deployment studies). If your interests fit one of these,
    or you have a related pitch of your own, we want to hear from you.
  </p>
</section>
